Our Group

Simply Strings is a string quartet in Jacksonville/Ponte Vedra. We perform for many events across town and beyond. We meet weekly to plan and practice for our upcoming performances and events. Each of us has performed and practiced in many different ways, whether through ensembles or school orchestras.

Madison

My name is Madison and I have been playing the violin since I was 4 years old. I started playing viola 2 years ago. I also have 8 years of youth orchestra experience. I am currently a freshman at Douglas Anderson School of the Arts and have been playing with Simply Strings for 2 years.

Hazel

My name is Hazel. I began my musical training at the age of three, and continued my studies with some of the best teachers and musicians in North Florida. Having the opportunity to be part of JSYO - Jacksonville Symphony Youth Orchestra for 6 years and performed as violinist and cellist at several local venues. I am currently attending Douglas Anderson School of the Arts.
