Our Group

Simply Strings is a group of violin and cello students in Ponte Vedra, Florida. We perform for many events across town and beyond. We meet weekly to plan and practice for our upcoming performances and events. Each of us has performed and practiced in many different ways, whether through orchestra ensembles or school bands.

Emily

My name is Emily and I play the cello for Simply Strings. I’ve been playing since 4th grade and have played with many groups since then. I’m excited to have co-founded this group with some of my peers and have loved playing with them so far!

Natalie

I'm Natalie, one of the co-founders of Simply Strings. I've been playing violin since I was 6 years old, and I'm super excited to further this experience with these girls! I've played in many orchestras and served as background music in weddings, parties and dinners throughout the years.

Madison

I'm Madison and I've been playing the violin for 10 years and recently started playing the viola. I go to LaVilla School of the Arts, and I'm very excited to be a part of Simply Strings.

Anne

My name is Anne, and I’ve been playing the violin for 9 years. I founded Simply Strings with a few of my friends and have now moved on to college where I hope to continue to play the violin!
